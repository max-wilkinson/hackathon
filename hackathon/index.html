House Rules

  • Team sizes between 3-7 people are recommended.
  • Leverage the diverse skills of a balanced team. Extra points will be granted to teams with diverse roles (Product Manager, Software Engineer, Product Designer, Architect, etc...)
  • Be prepared with your laptops, chargers, and sticky notes.
  • Keep track of time, so that you can deliver a product by the end of the event.
  • Have fun!

Judging

Each team will have 5 minutes to showcase their idea to the judges.

Judges will select the top 5 teams to present to the group.

Of the top 5 teams, two will be declared winners.
